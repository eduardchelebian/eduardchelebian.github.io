About me

I am a 3rd year PhD candidate supervised by Carolina Wahlby at the Information Technology Department of Uppsala University, Sweden. I obtained my BSc and MSc in Biomedical Engineering at the Universitat Politècnica de València, Spain, where I worked with Juan Miguel García Gómez at the Biomedical Data Science Lab.
During my PhD, I have done two internships: (1) at the Diagnostic Image Analysis Group in Radboud University Medical Center (RadboudUMC), The Netherlands under the supervision of Francesco Ciompi and (2) at the Genomics Laboratory in RIKEN, Japan under the supervision of Chung Chau Hon.
